Meow is a native iOS Clash-compatible proxy client powered by the Mihomo engine, wrapped in a SwiftUI interface and a NetworkExtension packet tunnel.

Bring your own subscription — Meow does not provide proxy servers.

Features
• Clash / Mihomo rule engine with full configuration support
• Subscription import via URL or clipboard, with one-tap refresh
• Supported outbound protocols: Shadowsocks, Trojan, and VLESS
• Per-rule routing, GeoIP/ASN aware decisions, lazy database download
• Encrypted Client Hello (ECH) via boring-tls
• System-wide tunnel via Apple's NetworkExtension framework
• Optimised for low memory: stays within the iOS extension footprint cap
• Universal app for iPhone and iPad, iOS 17 or later

Privacy
Meow stores your configuration on-device. It does not collect telemetry, advertising identifiers, or browsing history. Network traffic is forwarded to the proxy servers in your own subscription — Apple and the developer never see it.

Notes
This app is intended for users who already operate or subscribe to a Clash / Mihomo compatible proxy service. It is not a VPN provider. UDP and QUIC behavior depends on the upstream server's support.

Source code, documentation, and the public TestFlight beta are available on GitHub: https://github.com/madeye/meow-ios
